Create Your Own Mind Map with Mindly.ai

Welcome to Mindly.ai , the AI-powered tool that transforms the way you learn. With Mindly.ai, you can effortlessly create your own mind maps from your study materials. Whether it's lecture notes or textbooks Mindly.ai analyzes your documents to extract key concepts and visualize their connections.

Get Started

Turn complex ideas into clear, structured mind maps, powered by Azure AI.

Our intuitive platform makes it easy to organize your thoughts, understand complex topics, and improve memory retention. Simply upload your documents, and Mindly.ai will automatically generate a detailed mind map, highlighting the relationships between different ideas. You can export your mind maps, making it a valuable resource for study groups or personal reviews.

Examples

Development • SEP 1, 2024

Circular Node Layout: Focused and Organized Mind Mapping

This view presents the mind map using a circular node layout, combining a hierarchical tree structure with visually distinct, circular elements. The circular nodes draw attention to each topic individually, making it ideal for situations where you want to emphasize each point while maintaining a clear overall structure.

Development • SEP 1, 2024

Radial Layout View: Centralized Idea Exploration

The radial layout view displays the mind map with the main idea at the center, surrounded by branches of subtopics. This view is perfect for brainstorming sessions, where you can explore multiple related ideas radiating from a single central theme.

Development • SEP 1, 2024

Horizontal Tree Layout: Streamlined Information Flow

This view arranges the mind map in a horizontal tree layout, providing a streamlined way to visualize hierarchical relationships with a clear, left-to-right flow. It's perfect for presenting processes, sequences, or organizational structures where understanding the order or level of each element is crucial.
